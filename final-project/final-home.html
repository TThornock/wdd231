My Goal

My goal is to share my experiences traveling as someone recently diagnosed with celiac disease. Traveling can be challenging with dietary restrictions, but I hope my experiences help others who face the same challenges.

Questions

  • What is celiac?: It is an autoimmune disorder triggered by gluten which is found in many common grains like wheat, barley, and rye.
  • How do I find safe food?: I recomend doing research online and ask staff at the location you wish to try about gluten precautions.
  • Have I reacted to “safe” places?: Yes—some “gluten-friendly” places still risk contamination so always check with the server.
